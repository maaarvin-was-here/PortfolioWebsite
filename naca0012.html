NACA 0012 Airfoil Demonstration in ANSYS Fluent

A more detailed write-up of this project is in progress, as well as a follow-up in which I demonstrate the effects of winglets on airflow, testing both blended and split-scimitar winglets.

This was just a quick little project to practice using ANSYS Fluent. I tested an NACA 0012 airfoil placed at a 6 degree angle of attack in a wind tunnel. I used a medium-fine mesh under standard conditions and collected data on pressure and velocity at different points on and around the airfoil surface.
