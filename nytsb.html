New York Times Spelling Bee Game

During my senior year of high school, I became obsessed with the New York Time's Spelling Bee, a game that challenges players to make as many words as they can with a daily bank of seven letters. As a thrifty high school student, I didn't want to spend excess $$ on a NYT subscription, which is required to access the game. This motivated me to take on this project, a webscraped version of the game that scrapes the daily letters directly from the NYT website. Try it here!!
